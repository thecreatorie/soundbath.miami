Design Notes for Framer Build

Photography Direction

When imagery is ready: close-up textures of stone and water, steam rising, warm amber light. No faces in the hero — keep it abstract and sensory. People can appear further down the page once trust is established. Think thermal bath photography from Vals Therme or Aman resorts.

Animation & Motion

Slow, intentional. Parallax scroll on the hero image. Text fades in gently on scroll — nothing bounces or slides. The pace of the site should feel like being in the water: unhurried. Consider a subtle water ripple or steam animation on hover for the CTA.

Orange Usage

Orange appears only on interactive elements and moments of emphasis: the CTA button, the "Coming to Miami" label, and text accents. Never as a background fill for large sections. It should feel like a warm glow — the ember, not the fire. Use sparingly so it draws the eye exactly where you want it.

Framer Template Direction

Search Framer templates for: luxury hotel, resort, wellness, or architecture portfolios. Look for templates with dark hero sections, editorial typography, and generous whitespace. Customize the palette to these colors. Avoid anything with bright gradients, rounded playful buttons, or tech-startup energy.
